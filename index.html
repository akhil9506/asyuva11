About Yuva

Welcome to Andhra Samithi

Do you know what differentiates us from one another? Its expression of our feelings. Shortly known as art. A coconut looks very hard and tough from outside but has sweet water hidden inside. The same way today's person is deeply involved in scientific world. But there is an art hidden in each and every person. The art that lacks courage to come out. The art that doesn't have proper stage to showcase. This fest is for that hidden art. This fest is to awaken that art. This fest is to encourage that art. This fest is to showcase that art. This fest is to respect that art
